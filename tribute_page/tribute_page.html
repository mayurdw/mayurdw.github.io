Oscar Schindler

For those who have not seen 'Schindler's List' movie, stop reading this and watch it now!

Here are some excerpts from his website:

  • Oscar Schindler saved more than 1200 Jews. He was all that stood between them and death at the hands of the Nazis.
  • Oscar Schindler and his wife Emilie Schindler were inspiring evidence of courage and human decency during the Holocaust.
  • Today there are more than 7,000 descendants of the Schindler-Jews living in US and Europe, many in Israel.
  • Oscar Schindler spent millions to protect and save his Jews, everything he possessed. He died penniless.
  • Schindler died in Hildesheim in Germany October 9, 1974
